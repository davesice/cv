David Thor Gudmundsson

GIS Analyst / Developer at Arup

Along with a strong sense and interest for GIS and Information Technology, it is within the area of city layouts and city views David Thor's passion and appreciation lies. This comes along with his newly found interest in dealing with innovative transportation / interchange solutions in urban environment. Over the last few years David has managed three garden design projects from design to completion, as well as completing a master’s degree in Geospatial Technologies, in Lisbon, Portugal and Munster, Germany and a postgraduate diploma in landscape architecture in London, UK and is currently employed as a GIS Analyst with a London based firm.

By living in various natural and cultural environments: Iceland, Norway, Portugal, Germany and now the UK as well as being a travel enthusiast with a critical eye how the wheels turn within different communities, David Thor makes sure his creative sense is constantly being challenged. David Thor has a full working suite environment from home for remote contracts, through his Iceland based company DDX, as well as offering site visits when needed. GIS services provided include (but not limited to): Data gathering (open source / purchased), map making of any sort, geostatistical analysis, viewshed and spatial analysis, and data analysis with statistical representations.
